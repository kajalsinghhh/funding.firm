Daily Drawdown limit - 5%
  • You can't make a loss of exactly or more than 5% on your principal amount in a single day.
  • For example, if you have an account of $100,000, $ 100000 x 5% = $5000 So if you make a loss of $5000 or do floating, your account will be disabled
  • If you book a profit of $5000 on your $100000 account, making it to $105000, then $105,000 x 5% = $5250 will be your daily loss limit.
  • If you book a loss of $4000 on your $100000 account, making it to $96000, then $96,000 x 5% = $4800 will be your daily loss limit.
Overall Drawdown limit -12%
  • An overall drawdown means a loss limit of 12%.
  • For example, $100000 x 12% = $12000 $100000 - $12000 = $88000
  • You need to keep your account above $88100; if it goes below $88,100 or it is $88,000, your account will be suspended due to a rule breach.

Be Transparent :

You Are prohibited from Doing any of these kind of activities;

  • High-Frequency Trading (HFT) : In other words, using computer algorithms or automated trading systems that will execute many trades extremely fast to profit from small price differences
  • Manipulative Trading : It doesn’t sound great, does it? Manipulative trading describes any form of “playing” the markets, i.e., tactics like spoofing (placing fake orders) or layering (multiple orders) to create an illusion of demand or supply). In other words, the rule is that you do not create fake market conditions or deceive the market neighbours!
  • Insider Trading : It should go without saying, but insider trading is strictly prohibited by prop firms. For those new to the term, insider trading refers to the use of material obtained before it is made public to have an unfair advantage. Insider trading is in fact illegal and can have prison consequences at escalated levels.
  • Arbitrage Violations : Confusingly, this does not simply refer to an “arbitrary violation” but technically the term means the use of random strategies that exploit price discrepancies. Some prop firms will authorise this technique, but usually, traders will need to proceed with prior permission from the firm in question.
  • Using any SPIDERS, EA or any Unfair Means Which is Not Ethical practice can Leads to suspension of your account.
Consistency Rules

We FundedFirm Ensure that traders maintain consistent trading volumes and activity levels, promoting disciplined trading behavior and effective risk management.

Rule Outline :
1. Weekly Lot Consistency :
  • Traders must maintain an average weekly lot size within a 10% variance of the previous week's average.
  • For example, if a trader averages 5 lots in a week, the following week’s average should be between 4.5 and 5.5 lots.
2. Minimum Trading days :
  • The trader must trade consistently over a minimum of 5 Trading Days within a calendar month to qualify for evaluation.
  • Each week must meet the above consistency criteria.
3. Scaling Factor for Volume Growth :
  • If a trader seeks to increase their trading volume, they must notify FundedFirm and demonstrate gradual scaling. The trader can only increase their average weekly lot size by up to 15% after maintaining consistency for four consecutive weeks.
4. Risk and Lot Management :
  • Traders are encouraged to manage their lot sizes relative to their account balance. A sudden, significant increase in lot size without proper notification or justification may lead to a review of their account.
  • Any significant deviation in lot size or trade count without prior communication may result in a warning or disqualification from payouts for that period.
5. Profit Distribution :
  • A trader must not make more than 30% of their total profits from a single trade.
5(1). Violation Consequences :
  • A deviation beyond the allowed variance without proper notification may result in a 50% reduction in profit split for that month.
  • Repeated violations over two consecutive Weeks may lead to a mandatory trading behavior review and possible account suspension.
Rationale :

This rule encourages traders to maintain steady and predictable trading volumes, which helps in managing risk and ensuring that profits are earned through consistent trading behavior. It also offers room for growth, allowing traders to scale their volumes over time while maintaining discipline.
